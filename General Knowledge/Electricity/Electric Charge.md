Prerequisites: [[Matter]], [[Atom]]

Recall that every atom can have a different number of protons, neutrons and electrons.

Electric charge is a property that matter has.

Protons have a positive charge, neutrons have a neutral charge and electrons have a negative charge.
- If an object has equal protons and neutrons it has a neutral charge.
- If an object has more protons that electrons, it has a net positive charge
- If an object has more electrons that protons it has a net negative charge

Like charges repel and opposite charges attract. E.g. two electrons acting on each other without any other forces will repel.

A coulomb (C) is the unit of electric charge.

1 coulomb is the amount of charge carried by approximately 6.242 × 10¹⁸ electrons.
