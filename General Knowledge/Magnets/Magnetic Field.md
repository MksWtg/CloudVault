Prerequisite: [[Electric Charge]], [[Electric Field]]

A physical property of space that determines the force experienced by a moving electric charge.

> Ordinary electric fields act on stationary charges. When a charge moves, it is under the effects of the electric field plus magnetic fields.

If a particle with electric charge $q$ moves with velocity $v$ through a field $B$ it experiences a force $F$:
$$F = q v \times B$$
