Prerequisites: [[Electric Charge]], [[Magnetic Field]], [[Magnetic Moment]]

A magnet is a physical object, made of a material whose internal electric charges are arranged so that they produce a magnetic field.

![[Pasted image 20260301152646.png]]
Inside matter electrons have a property called spin, and we know electrons have charge. Spinning electric charges create magnetic fields, so every electron is like a tiny magnet. In most materials, these effects cancel out randomly. In magnets, the magnetic moments align in the same direction so their effects add together. This produces a stable magnetic field.
