Prerequisites: [[Magnets]], [[Magnetic Field]], [[Magnetic Moment]]

Materials are not magnetic by default. This means they cannot be moved by magnets/magnetic fields, because they do not have a net magnetic moment.
- every atom has a small magnetic moment
- they point randomly
- they cancel out

Materials that are magnetic are made of ferromagnetic materials

- [[Diamagnetism]]
