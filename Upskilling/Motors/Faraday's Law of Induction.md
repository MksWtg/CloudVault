Prerequisites: [[Voltage]], [[Magnetic Flux]], [[Circuit]], [[Lenz's Law]], [[Electromagnetic Induction]]

The voltage induced in a circuit is proportional to the rate of change of magnetic flux through the circuit.

This makes the formula

$$\epsilon = -N \frac{d \phi} {d t}$$

Where 
$\epsilon$ is the EMF
$N$ is the number of turns of the coil
$\frac{d\phi}{dt}$ is the rate of change of magnetic flux

The negative sign comes from Lenz's law
