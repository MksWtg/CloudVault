The laws of electromagnetism describe how electric charges and magnetic fields interact.

1) Gauss' Law for Electricity: Charge's create electric fields e.g. capacitor
2) Gauss' Law for Magnetism: Moving charges create magnetic fields e.g. a current in a wire creates a magnetic field
3) Faraday's Law of Induction: A changing magnetic field generates an electric field (voltage)
4) Ampere-Maxwell Law: Changing electric fields generate a magnetic field

Table:

|Cause|Effect|
|---|---|
|Charge|Electric field|
|Moving charge (current)|Magnetic field|
|Changing magnetic field|Electric field|
|Changing electric field|Magnetic field|
