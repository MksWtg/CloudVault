
Over the last year we have been using AI across many parts of the business, with this accelerating since December 2025. Recently, I spoke about the WiseTech AI Awards.

I’ve been very encouraged by the number of people asking for more details and wanting to submit their work, so today I am pleased to officially announce this program. 

AI is already changing how we work at WiseTech. We’re seeing many people realize that we must change our workflows, across all functions, to be AI-first or AI-led, rather than AI-last or AI-assisted. 

That means reimagining our work with AI involved from the start, with AI doing large parts of the work, and with human input where it is needed. We have passed the stage of simply using AI to re-write our emails or perform clean-up after we’ve done the work ourselves.

This is the way we deliver on our goals; this is how we succeed and how we truly become an AI-first organization.

These Awards are about recognizing those who have shown significant strides in this mission and sharing it more broadly so that we can all learn from each other as we work towards our AI-first approach.

What we’re looking for  
Whether you’re in a non-technical role and just starting to experiment or have been working in this space for some time, I encourage you to put your work forward. 

Some examples that you may want to include:
Practical AI that improves throughput by automating work, accelerating analysis or innovation, improving decision quality, or reducing cycle time. 
AI innovation in products, engineering, or internal systems that creates new capability, including approaches that can scale and unlock follow-on work.
Cross-functional outcomes where technical and non-technical teams work together to solve meaningful problems, with clear shared ownership and real-world adoption.
Moving an AI initiative from idea to reality through pilots, iteration, and early adoption, with measurable progress and a credible path to scale.

How to enter 
You can enter as an individual or as a team up to 5 people. Visit the ​WiseTech AI Awards page​ on The Source for details on how to enter, key dates, and judging criteria.

Good ideas don’t need to be perfect. If you’ve improved something, solved a problem, or tried a new approach, submit it.  
 
What’s next 
The competition will be held in ongoing rounds, the first of which opens today. The closing date for round one will be Friday 24 April.

At the conclusion of each round, winning entries will be recognized. Each individual, including every team member if submitted as part of a team, will receive $1,000 AUD and the chance to win a larger prize to be announced later this year.
I’ll talk more about the Awards at Cake Day on Thursday.

I’ve already seen some amazing things built by people and teams across many functions of the business. Submit these! And submit more! 

I’m very excited to see what you all come up with.
